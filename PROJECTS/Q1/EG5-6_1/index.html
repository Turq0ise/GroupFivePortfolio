    How will I find the need of viewers in a streaming category?

  • What, How, and Why
  • 5 Whys
  • Assume a Beginner’s Mindset

    • a. What, How, and Why:

    Question: Why do people watch naruto?

    What: What makes Naruto unique?

    How: How did Naruto become well known?

    Why: Why is Naruto important to us?

    b. 5 Whys:

    Question: Why do people watch naruto?

    Answer: Because people want entertainment

    Why: Why do they want entertainment?

    Why: Why are they bored?

    Why: Why do people have lots of spare time?

    Why: Why don’t they have anything to do?

    Root Cause: People have nothing to do.
